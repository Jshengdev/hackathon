# Demo Script — 90 seconds, second-by-second

## PRE-DEMO CONTEXT (not on timer)

> Gen-Z has never thought freely. They were born on the algorithm. We fed their Instagram Reels through TRIBE V2 — Meta FAIR's brain-encoding model — and rebuilt their neurological response as a visible, clickable 3D architecture. The demo shows 90 seconds of their actual feed scrolling, live-rendered as brain activations, swarm-analyzed in real-time, and offered back as a Spotify-Wrapped-style of their thinking. The hero moment: they click a grayed-out brain region and see the content that would fire it — not recommended by the algorithm, but **suggested by their own neural landscape.**
>
> *"You can't see how the algorithm shapes your thinking. We made it visible."*

### Friday-night smoke-test gate (per Pre-mortem)

If any of these fail Friday 8-11 PM:
1. TRIBE latency baseline > 30s on demo GPU → BEAT-1 must be pre-cached
2. K2 swarm load test (10 concurrent specialist calls) shows timeouts → BEAT-2 must be pre-cached
3. 3D rendering FPS < 30 on demo laptop → mesh decimation or pre-cache
4. Wi-Fi contingency test fails → full backup MP4 required
