# 010 — B2C-primary, B2B-overlay positioning

## Decision

Positioning is **B2C-primary with a B2B-overlay**. One architecture, two surfaces, one honest problem statement that rings true to all 5 sponsor lenses simultaneously (no Frankenstein sponsor-track tacked-on language).

## Date locked

2026-04-25 (window-2 thesis snapshot articulation).

## Locked by

Johnny (verbatim, paraphrased into thesis snapshot):
> "Best Use of AI = redefining what proper AI use looks like in an unregulated, scary AI world. AI for the most people, used properly, ethically, sustainably — non-blackboxed so people can trust it. With B2C-primary + B2B-overlay positioning, and one honest problem statement that rings true to all 5 sponsors." [source: `research/wiki/themes/ai-paradox-invisible-use-cases/window-2-research-deepening/000-coordination-and-thesis-snapshot.md` line 17]

## Why

- **B2C-primary** is what wins HackTech's main "Best Use of AI" + "Creativity" tracks, AND what Sideshift wants ("build the best consumer app").
- **B2B-overlay** is what makes the same architecture sponsor-pitchable as a startup — what YC and (had it not been dropped) Palohouse care about.
- "One honest problem statement" forecloses the temptation to write 5 separately-pitched features per sponsor — that's the Frankenstein anti-pattern and judges sniff it.
- Mirrors the pattern in TreeHacks 2026 winners that swept multiple sponsor prizes — "they won them with one architectural shape that happened to satisfy several judges — they did not bolt five hooks on" [source: `501-party-mode-roundtable.md` Mara persona, line 49].

## What this opens

- B2C surface chosen first (open question 1 below); B2B angle reframes the same demo.
- Sponsor stacking under one architecture is feasible without contradicting any single sponsor's brief.
- Devpost / launch video / landing page can all be B2C-flavored (Emilie's "startup test" mandate).

## What this closes

- Pure-B2B infra pitches with no consumer surface — those don't win Sideshift, Best AI, Creativity universally.
- Pure-B2C without a defensible business pathway — those lose YC.
- Five separately-pitched features per sponsor (the Frankenstein anti-pattern).

## Reversibility

Reversible only if Sponsor talks Saturday surface a B2B-only judging eye that overrides the B2C frame. Currently no signal.

## Open questions (not resolved per protocol)

- **SC1 from `501-party-mode-roundtable.md`** + **anchor doc item 5:** What is the B2C surface, specifically? (Browser extension / standalone app / wearable / OS-layer / creator tool.) — Johnny's call.
- **D2 from `501-party-mode-roundtable.md`** (Sasha vs Mara cross-persona): Is the demo theatre moment visual (heatmap, citation chip, toggle) or audible (Auditor's voice disagreeing with Actor's voice in real time)? Both are recognition-shape; very different products.
- **S1 from `501-party-mode-roundtable.md`** (Mara): Of the five sponsor hooks, which three are non-negotiable and which two are nice-to-have? — sponsor-stacking vs. demo-clarity tension.
