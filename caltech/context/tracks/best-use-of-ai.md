# Track: Best Use of AI

## Official
- **Prize:** Mac Mini M4 16GB (provided by Extraordinary)
- **Description:** None given — generic "best AI" award
- **Sponsor:** Extraordinary (hardware sponsor)

## What They ACTUALLY Want (read between lines)
- This is the **universal track** — every project is scored on the AI rubric in Round 1 regardless of which other track it's in. So "Best AI" is decided across the entire hackathon pool, not by self-selection.
- "Best Use of AI" — emphasis on **USE**, not "most AI" or "biggest model". Judges are looking for AI that *unlocks something otherwise impossible*, not LLM wrappers.
- Mac Mini M4 from "Extraordinary" — sponsor brand likely values **clever, demo-able, polished applications** over deep research.
- Likely overlapping bias from sponsor talks (YC, Listen Labs, IFM, Ironsite) — judges have been steeped in agentic / reasoning / simulation discourse all weekend. Lean into those frames.

## How to Win
- AI must be **load-bearing** — remove the AI and the product breaks
- Visible reasoning loop or agentic behavior > one-shot prompt
- Live demo with real data > scripted/canned demo
- Frame the "wow moment" tightly so the 3-min Round 2 pitch lands

## Competition Level
- **Highest** — every team is implicitly competing for this, since AI rubric is universal. Need to differentiate via execution polish, novel UX, or non-obvious problem domain.

## Track Stacking
- Composes with literally every other track (it's universal)
- Strongest combos: Best AI + YC, Best AI + Listen Labs, Best AI + Ironsite, Best AI + IFM K2
