# Sponsor Insights

**What sponsors ACTUALLY reward — captured from real conversations and judging.**

## What You'll Find Here

Real insights from sponsor table conversations, judging sessions, and past winners:
- **Official criteria vs reality** — What they say vs what they actually want
- **Hidden priorities** — Unspoken preferences that affect judging
- **API usage patterns** — How to integrate their tech effectively
- **Bonus prize criteria** — Extra awards and how to win them
- **Judge backgrounds** — Who judges and what they care about
- **Past winner patterns** — What won their track historically

## Organization

### By Sponsor Company
Each file documents a specific sponsor:
- Company name
- Typical tracks they sponsor

### By Track Type
Cross-sponsor patterns by track:
- AI/ML tracks: What all AI sponsors care about

## Common Patterns

**What sponsors SAY:**
- "Be creative!"
- "Use our API"
- "Solve real problems"

**What sponsors ACTUALLY reward:**
- Specific use of their latest features
- Solutions to THEIR pain points
- Technical depth in their domain
- Proof of concept that could scale

**The Gap in sponsor priorities:**
- They want "production-ready" vibes, not perfect code
- They care more about vision than current features
- They remember projects that understood their product deeply
- They reward moonshot thinking in their domain

## Using Sponsor Insights

**Pre-hackathon:**
1. Research sponsors attending
2. Check if library has insights on them
3. Study their patterns
4. Plan questions for their table

**At hackathon:**
1. Visit sponsor tables with the 6 questions
2. Add insights to library immediately after conversations
3. Tag with hackathon name and year
4. Make searchable for future hacks

**During ideation:**
1. Search library for relevant sponsors
2. Align idea with their hidden priorities
3. Plan API integration approach
4. Identify bonus prize opportunities

**Build strategy:**
The library compounds over time. Each hackathon adds more sponsor insights. Eventually, you know what every major sponsor rewards before you even arrive.
