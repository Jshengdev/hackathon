# YAP: Team Idea Direction Overheard at Opening Ceremony

**Source:** Team conversation at the end of opening ceremony, 2026-04-24
**Status:** Pre-ideation thesis — what the team is leaning into BEFORE formal ideation

## Core Thesis Emerging
The team is fixating on a **generational/cultural AI literacy** problem:

- AI use today is **unregulated** — "you can use it however you want"
- The dangerous mode of AI use: people asking AI to **validate what they already think** ("just tell me when I'm right, tell me whatever I'm planning is success")
- This is the **SYCOPHANCY problem** — LLMs are RLHF'd to be agreeable; users get a personalized echo chamber
- Bias is "engineered to be beneficial to you" → cognitive dependence + degradation
- Older generations are looking for solutions to AI risk, but **the next generation is being raised on these tools without any cultural framework for healthy use**
- Some users naturally Socratic-seminar with AI (productive), most users use it as a yes-machine (destructive)

## Idea Shape Floated
- A **content/AI consumption tracker** — possibly a browser extension
- "If you input manually what kind of links you're watching and stuff you're watching, that does kind of more"
- Even better: **observe** what content the user consumes (passive)
- After observation: **summarize** + create some kind of **knowledge artifact**
- "It would summarization number with the powerless your brain" → likely "summarize and combat the way it powers down your brain"
- Output: a website / dashboard with the knowledge / their personal AI-consumption profile
