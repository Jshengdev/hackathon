# Andy Raskin — The Greatest Sales Deck I've Ever Seen

**Source:** Andy Raskin, Mission.org / Medium, 2016. https://medium.com/the-mission/the-greatest-sales-deck-ive-ever-seen-4f4ef3391ba0

**One-line claim:** Pitch decks should "tease the Promised Land" — show the desired future state — *before* introducing product details.

**Why we cited it:** Used in our demo-theatre canonical (§2.4 Hope, §3.10 Promised Land tease) as the framework for the **"Land" beat** of the 90-second arc. Renaissance Research won Best Use of AI 2025 in part by showing the consensus-ranked output (the Promised Land — *"papers worth re-trying today"*) before the architecture was explained.

**Five-step structure Raskin names:**

1. Name a Big, Relevant Change in the World
2. Show There'll Be Winners and Losers
3. Tease the Promised Land
4. Introduce features as "magic gifts" for overcoming obstacles
5. Present evidence that you can make the story come true

**For our arc:** Step 3 (Promised Land tease) maps to the demo's Land beat — showing the user being more themselves at the end of using the product. In our locked architecture: *the Spotify-Wrapped-of-your-brain card showing "your authentic brain-fire pattern vs. your algorithmic brain-fire pattern"*.
